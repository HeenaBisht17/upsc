üìù Teaching Exam Syllabus

The syllabus for teaching exams generally includes Child Development & Pedagogy, Language (I & II), Mathematics, Environmental Studies, General Knowledge, Reasoning, and Subject-Specific knowledge. The structure and weightage vary by exam.

‚ú® Common Subjects:

  • ‚óè
    Child Development & Pedagogy: Principles of child development, learning theories, inclusive education, understanding diverse learners, teaching-learning process.
  • ‚óè
    Language I & II: Comprehension, grammar, and pedagogy of language development (usually Hindi/English or regional languages).
  • ‚óè
    Mathematics: Number system, arithmetic, geometry, data handling, and pedagogical issues in teaching mathematics.
  • ‚óè
    Environmental Studies (EVS): Family and friends, food, shelter, water, travel, things we make and do, and pedagogical issues related to EVS.
  • ‚óè
    General Knowledge & Current Affairs: National and international events, history, geography, polity, economy, science.
  • ‚óè
    Reasoning Ability: Verbal and non-verbal reasoning, analytical reasoning, problem-solving.
  • ‚óè
    Subject-Specific Knowledge: For TGT/PGT level exams, in-depth knowledge of the chosen teaching subject.
