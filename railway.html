üìù Railways Exam Syllabus

The syllabus for most Railway exams generally includes General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness, Mathematics, and General Science. The emphasis and depth of each subject vary based on the specific exam.

‚ú® Common Subjects:

  • ‚óè
    General Intelligence & Reasoning: Analogies, Syllogism, Data Sufficiency, Coding-Decoding, Venn Diagrams, Non-Verbal Reasoning, etc.
  • ‚óè
    General Awareness: Current Affairs, Indian History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Art & Culture, Sports, General Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology).
  • ‚óè
    Mathematics: Number System, BODMAS, Decimals, Fractions, LCM, HCF, Ratio and Proportion, Percentage, Mensuration, Time and Work, Time and Distance, Simple and Compound Interest, Profit and Loss, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Elementary Statistics.
